🌿 Option A

Old Town Revisit + Harney Sushi + Sweet Finish

Familiar in the best way — sunny, browse-y, and easy

Time

🌹 The Vibe

This option feels familiar in a good way. A sunny Old Town start gives you both some shape before dinner without making the day feel over-planned. Harney Sushi becomes the intentional centerpiece instead of a last-minute choice. Then an easy dessert-style finish nearby so the night can end without extra hassle — close to where you parked.

1

Old Town Wandering

Old Town San Diego State Historic Park

Google Maps Apple Maps Park Info
sunny • relaxed • browse-y

A slow Old Town start could work really well here because it gives the date some shape before dinner without making the day feel over-planned. The state park area is centered around San Diego Avenue and Twiggs Street, and the district is built for walkability and daytime exploring.

What this could feel like: A gentle loop through the historic core, a little shopping, a little people-watching, and just enough walking that dinner feels like the second chapter of the date instead of the whole thing.

✨ You might like:

  • Bazaar del Mundo — Perfect for the “walk around and look at cute things” part of the afternoon. Colorful, browse-worthy, and right in the heart of Old Town.
📋 Backup Plan

Heritage County Park could be a quieter version of the same start if you want less foot traffic.

2

Harney Sushi Dinner

3964 Harney St, San Diego, CA 92110

(619) 295-3272

Google Maps Apple Maps Call Menu Reserve on OpenTable
lively • familiar-favorite • date-night

Dinner at Harney Sushi makes a lot of sense here because it already feels meaningful to you both, but the rest of the plan around it helps it feel like a full date — not just dinner with a side activity. The restaurant has general street parking, valet, and a small lot in the back.

Hours: Sunday 4:00 PM – 10:00 PM

Atmosphere: Lively and fun — great for a date night where you want energy but still want to hear each other.

🍽 What to Order

Pick your lane — or mix and match!

✨ Brighter, Cleaner, Lighter
  • Andy 2 Roll
  • Protein Roll
  • Avocado Roll or Hippie Roll

Citrusy, fresher, and a little less heavy.

🔥 Bolder, Spicier, More Playful
  • Culichi Wa Roll
  • Crunch Roll
  • All 4 Edamame (shared appetizer)

The “fun sushi date” version of dinner.

🧀 Lactose Note

A few specialty rolls at Harney do include cream cheese or creamier sauces. Sticking closer to the cleaner fish-forward and cucumber-wrap side of the menu would probably make this easier for the lactose issue.

📋 Backup Plan

If Harney feels louder than expected, dinner could shift within the neighborhood without breaking the date.

2

La Jolla Village & Cove Walk

Ellen Browning Scripps Park / La Jolla Cove

Google Maps Apple Maps
scenic • browse-y • romantic

This is the part that gives the date that “walk somewhere pretty after trying something new” feeling. The cove area is a public shoreline park, and the village is central to walking between dining, shopping, and the coast.

What this could feel like: A village stroll, a stop or two to browse, then a water-view walk before dinner.

✨ You might like:

  • Browse the village shops along Prospect St and Girard Ave
  • Walk down to the cove for ocean views and sea lions
  • Ellen Browning Scripps Park for a grassy, scenic pause
📋 Backup Plan

If the coastline feels busier than you want, the village streets can still carry this middle section nicely.

Roppongi fits your food profile really well because it gives you Asian flavors, seafood, tapas-style sharing, and a stronger date-night atmosphere. A lot of the dishes naturally sit outside the heavy-dairy zone, which makes the lactose issue easier to work around.

🔥 Bolder, Spicier, More Playful

📋 Backup Plan

If you wanted to keep the same La Jolla flow but pivot the meal, the village has enough density that the structure of the date still works.
